Resturant Description

Lord loafs is a sandwich spot that prides it self on baking the most fresh breads on the planet. Breads are baked fresh daily and range in options. These options include Brioche, Rye, Honey Oat, Croissant, Naan, Sour Dough and the English Muffin. We also pride ourselves in having healthier alternatives like Sesame Sprouted Whole Grain and our gluten and yeast free loaves. All menu options are paired with the bread of your choice.
